MTRNM Presents: VXSION at 77 London 
/ˈvɪʒ(ə)n/ [noun]
All the way from Brazil, VXSION touches down in London on February 7th. A rising force in global house, his sound blends deep grooves with Afro-house rhythms, earning support from Keinemusik, Black Coffee, and beyond.
From the Dinosaur Halls of the Natural History Museum in Los Angeles to the spectacle that is club 77, VXSION joins us once again for a night of good people, good vibes and some damn good music.
