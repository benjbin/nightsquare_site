CABO NEGRO X HABITO
On Friday May 15th, we arrive in London for an exclusive night at Paloma.

Known for creating immersive nights blending elegant House and Afro House sounds, Cabo Negro & Habito bring its unique energy to one of London’s most refined nightlife atmospheres. Expect deep rhythms, hypnotic melodies and an intimate crowd gathered for a night driven by music, connection and pure vibration.

From Marbella to London, this special edition promises a sophisticated experience where warm grooves, powerful energy and elevated aesthetics meet until late.
