MTRNM Presents: Night at the Courthouse ⚖️
/naɪt æt ðə ˈkɔːrtˌhaʊs/ [noun]
This March 28th, the Surrogate’s Courthouse in New York City opens its grand doors for the first edition of MTRNM’s newest landmark series — a night where verdicts are suspended, rhythms take control, and house music holds final jurisdiction. ️️
From 8PM–1AM, deep grooves and driving house rhythms echo through one of NYC’s most breathtaking historic landmarks, as the courthouse transforms into a dancefloor for one unforgettable night.
