Babylon & Joa Invite Orsay

JOA Beach Sxm · Saint-Martin

Date dimanche 15 mars 2026 · 00:00
Lieu JOA Beach Sxm, Saint-Martin
Musique Afro House · Tech House

On Saturday, March 14, BABYLON JOA and welcome ORSAY. Support by Zac & Leo For the very first time in Saint Martin, Babylon brings the pulse of Parisian nightlife to the island. Renowned for its iconic events in Paris and across major European capitals, Babylon has hosted standout artists such as Paloma Lefriant, Levi, Hugel, Mëstiza, Moojo, Marten Lou, and many other leading figures from the afro-house and electronic scene. Come discover the universe of Babylon, where creativity meets House music, for a unique show under the palm trees in St Maarten. Line Up : Orsay
